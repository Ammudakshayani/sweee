ABSTRACT :-

It is a survey conducted to find how many people are consuming nutritious food and junk food in an area. For this survey we selected Nethaji nagar colony, Kovur , in SPSR Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh. Our main theme is to know how many people are consuming nutritious food and junk food in the area and also explain the advantages of consuming nutritious food to the people who are unable to know which food is good for health. We collected the data from various people and found out how many people are well known about healthy diet in that area.
